Case Studies

01 Digital Banking

Digital Banking App

Problem — A challenger bank's onboarding funnel was losing 40% of users before completion. KYC steps felt bureaucratic, trust signals were absent, and error states caused abandonment.

Solution — Redesigned the full onboarding journey with progressive disclosure, real-time document validation feedback, and trust-building micro-copy at each friction point.

+18% Onboarding completion
−31% KYC abandonment
4.7★ App Store rating
02 AI / Enterprise

AI Call Centre Platform

Problem — Agents handling financial disputes lacked real-time context. Average handle time was 8 minutes; customer satisfaction was critically low during escalations.

Solution — Designed an AI-assisted agent dashboard that surfaces customer intent, sentiment, and suggested responses in real time — without overwhelming cognitive load.

−2.4m Avg handle time
+22% First contact resolution
+34% CSAT score
03 IoT / Mobile

Smart Home Mobile App

Problem — Users of a smart home platform couldn't understand their energy spend or act on it. The app showed data but provided no actionable insight, leading to disengagement.

Solution — Introduced an insight-first model: predictive alerts, one-tap automation rules, and an energy timeline that connects IoT data to real cost savings.

+45% Daily active usage
−25% Reported drop-off
3.1× Automation rule adoption

Process

Design Thinking

01

Problem Framing

Start with user behaviour data, not opinions. Map the job-to-be-done before touching Figma.

02

Decision Making

Design decisions anchored to metrics. Every choice has a hypothesis; hypotheses are validated.

03

Trade-offs

Speed vs. depth. Delight vs. compliance. I make the trade-off explicit and choose deliberately.
